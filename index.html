Introduction

CapCut is a free, all-in-one video editing app that helps you create incredible videos with ease. This documentation will guide you through the main features and functions of CapCut.

Features

  • Trimming and Splitting
  • Adding Text and Titles
  • Applying Filters and Effects
  • Transitions between clips
  • Audio editing with music and sound effects

How to Start

To start using CapCut, download the app on your mobile device from the App Store or Google Play. Once installed, open the app and create a new project by tapping the “+ New Project” button.

Editing Tools

Trimming and Splitting

Use the trimming tool to cut out unwanted sections of your video. Select the clip, drag the edges, or split it at specific points.

Adding Text

To add text, tap on the “Text” option, choose from various fonts and styles, and adjust its position and timing on the timeline.

Applying Filters

CapCut offers multiple filters to give your videos a unique look. Tap on “Filters” to browse and apply them to your clips.

Exporting

Once you’ve finished editing, tap on the “Export” button at the top right corner. Choose your preferred resolution, and CapCut will save your video to your device.
